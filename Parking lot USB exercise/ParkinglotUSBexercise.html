Parking lot USB exercise

Contents

Write 2-3 sentences about the types of information found on this device.

  • Are there files that can contain PII?
  • Are there sensitive work files?
  • Is it safe to store personal files with work files?


There are files in the USB drive that contain PII information. Sensitive work files like employee shift schedules, employee budgets, resumes, and new hire letters contain vulnerable data of the hospital. Personal files and work files need to be stored separately as it allows the other party to gain access to data they are not supposed to access.

Attacker mindset

Write 2-3 sentences about how this information could be used against Jorge or the hospital.

  • Could the information be used against other employees?
  • Could the information be used against relatives?
  • Could the information provide access to the business?

The USB drive contains sensitive work files that contain other’s data. It also has folders containing personal data like family photos. These pieces of information can be exploited by an attacker to gain access to the business or manipulate someone in the family.

Risk analysis

Write 3 or 4 sentences describing technical, operational, or managerial controls that could mitigate these types of attacks:

  • What types of malicious software could be hidden on these devices? What could have happened if the device were infected and discovered by another employee?
  • What sensitive information could a threat actor find on a device like this?
  • How might that information be used against an individual or an organization?

Malicious software that can infect a device with malicious code could be hidden on the device. If the device was infected and was found by another employee, he could have connected the USB drive to his work computer or his personal computer, giving access to the threat actor. Moreover, a threat actor can use the information stored on the device to find vulnerabilities to manipulate someone personally or to manipulate the organization as a whole.
