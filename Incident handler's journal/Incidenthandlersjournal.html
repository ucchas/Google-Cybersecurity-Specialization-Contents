Date: 29/10/2023

Entry: #1

Description

A small U.S. healthcare clinic had a security incident on Tuesday, 9:00 a.m. which severely impacted their operations.

Tool(s) used

None

The 5 W's

Capture the 5 W's of an incident.

  • Who: An organized group of unethical hackers. An organized group of unethical hackers who are known to target who are known to attack healthcare and transportation organizations
  • What: A ransomware security incident. A phishing attack took place through the employee email ids. The email contained a malicious file containing malware that infected the devices upon installation. It was a ransomware attack.
  • When: The incident occurred on Tuesday at 9:00 a.m.
  • Where: At a health care company. The incident happened on the employee computers that the employees use to access the healthcare information of the patients
  • Why: The incident happened because unethical hackers were able to access the company's systems using a phishing attack. After gaining access, the attackers launched their ransomware on the company's systems, encrypting critical files. The attackers' motivation appears to be financial because the ransom note they left demanded a large sum of money in exchange for the decryption key.

The incident happened because there was a lack of awareness among the employees about various social engineering techniques that cybercriminals use to manipulate to install malware onto computers to gain sensitive information. A lack of active incident responders can also be a reason since there was no active sniffing of the emails for malicious files or intent.

Additional notes

  1. How could the health care company prevent an incident like this from occurring again?
  2. Should the company pay the ransom to retrieve the decryption key?

Training shall be conducted amongst employees to avoid these kinds of incidents in the future. Backup of all important files shall be maintained. Also to handle phishing attacks, active incident response efforts need to be implemented.
