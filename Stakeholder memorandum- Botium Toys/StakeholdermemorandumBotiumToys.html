TO: IT Manager, Stakeholders

FROM: Ucchas Muhury
DATE: 02/07/2023
SUBJECT: Internal IT Audit Findings and Recommendations

Dear Colleagues,

Please review the following information regarding the Botium Toys internal audit scope, goals, critical findings, summary and recommendations.

Scope:

Botium Toys internal IT audit will assess the following:

● Current user permissions, implemented controls, procedures and protocols set in the following systems: accounting, end point detection, firewalls, intrusion detection system, security information and event management (SIEM) tool.

● Ensure current user permissions, controls, procedures, and protocols in place

align with necessary compliance requirements.

● Ensure current technology is accounted for. Both hardware and system access.

Goals:

The goals for Botium Toys’ internal IT audit are:

● To adhere to the National Institute of Standards and Technology Cybersecurity

Framework (NIST CSF)

● Establish a better process for their systems to ensure they are compliant

● Fortify system controls

● Implement the concept of least permissions when it comes to user credential

management

● Establish their policies and procedures, which includes their playbooks

● Ensure they are meeting compliance requirements

Critical findings (must be addressed immediately):

Controls that need to be implemented immediately:
Administrative:

  • Least Privilege
  • Disaster Recovery Plans
  • Password policies
  • Access control policies
  • Separation of duties

Technical:

  • Intrusion Detection System(IDS)
  • Encryption
  • Backups
  • Antivirus(AV) software
  • Manual monitoring, maintenance, and intervention

Physical:

  • Closed-circuit television(CCTV) surveillance
  • Locks

Compliance that must be followed:

  1. General Data Protection Regulation(GDPR) [European Union]
  2. Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard(PCI DSS)
  3. System and Organizations Controls (SOC type 1, SOC type 2)

Findings (should be addressed, but no immediate need):

Controls that are of secondary importance:

Administrative:

  • Account management policies

Technical:

  • Password management system

Physical:

  • Time-controlled safe
  • Adequate lighting
  • Locking cabinets(for network gear)
  • Signage indicating alarm service provider
  • Fire detection and prevention(fire alarm, sprinkler system, etc.)

Summary/Recommendations: To maintain business continuity and ensure security and safety of the organization's assets and the people it serves, the compliance standards and control protocols obtained from the above findings need to be addressed. Additionally, adherence to the government/non-government regulations and internal protocols by the employees within the organization is of high importance and the employees may be educated as such.
